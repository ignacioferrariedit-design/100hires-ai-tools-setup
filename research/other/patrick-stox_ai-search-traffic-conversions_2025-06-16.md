# Does AI Search Traffic Convert Better Than Traditional Search?

* Author: Patrick Stox
* Organization: Ahrefs
* Content type: Public data study / research notes
* Original source URL: `https://ahrefs.com/blog/ai-search-traffic-conversions-ahrefs/`
* Publication date: 2025-06-16
* Collection method: Manual review of the public Ahrefs data study
* Collection date: 2026-06-15
* Research topic: AI-powered SEO content production for B2B SaaS

## Research Summary

Patrick Stox analyzes Ahrefs conversion data to compare traffic from AI assistants with traditional organic search. Although AI search represented a small share of total website visits, it contributed a disproportionately large share of product signups.

The findings suggest that AI-search visitors may arrive with stronger intent because they have already completed part of the research and comparison process inside tools such as ChatGPT before visiting the cited website.

## Key Findings

1. AI search accounted for approximately 0.5% of Ahrefs traffic during the analyzed 30-day period.
2. That traffic generated 12.1% of Ahrefs signups.
3. AI-search visitors converted approximately 23 times better than traditional organic-search visitors in this dataset.
4. ChatGPT generated most of the measured AI referral traffic and signups.
5. AI traffic volume remained comparatively small but was growing.
6. Traffic and conversion performance should be evaluated separately because a low-volume source may still produce valuable business outcomes.

## Implications for B2B SaaS

B2B SaaS companies should not evaluate AI visibility only through traffic volume. A visitor referred by an AI assistant may already understand the problem, know the available options, and arrive closer to a purchasing decision.

Content-production systems should therefore create material that helps AI tools explain:

* what the product does;
* which customer problem it solves;
* how it compares with alternatives;
* which use cases and integrations it supports;
* what evidence demonstrates credibility;
* what the visitor should do next.

Product pages, comparison pages, free tools, use-case pages, and clear homepage messaging may have significant value in AI-assisted buying journeys.

## Measurement Recommendations

Teams should track:

1. referral traffic from AI assistants;
2. signups and conversions by AI referral source;
3. landing-page type;
4. assisted conversions and downstream revenue;
5. branded and non-branded AI visibility;
6. citation and mention frequency.

AI Search performance should be connected to business outcomes rather than judged only through impressions or clicks.
