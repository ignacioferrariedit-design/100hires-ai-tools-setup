## Research Summary

Bernard Huang argues that AI-driven SEO should focus on building useful, connected knowledge rather than automatically producing large volumes of generic content. AI should help teams understand topic relationships, search intent, content gaps, and semantic structure, while humans remain responsible for expertise, originality, judgment, and brand voice.

## Key Insights

1. AI search evaluates connected knowledge, not only isolated keywords.
2. Content quantity does not create authority when the material is generic.
3. AI should support research, organization, structure, and topic coverage.
4. Human experts should provide experience, interpretation, originality, and final review.
5. SEO measurement should include citations and visibility in AI-generated answers.
6. Content should form interconnected topic ecosystems rather than isolated pages.

## Implications for B2B SaaS

B2B SaaS companies should use AI to map customer problems, product use cases, semantic gaps, and buying-journey questions. Content systems should preserve expert review, consistent terminology, internal linking, original product knowledge, and a clear relationship between each article and the broader product category.
